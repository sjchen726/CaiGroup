This folder is intended to hold image assets copied from `CEDER Group at Berkeley and LBL_files`.
Original images are located in `CEDER Group at Berkeley and LBL_files/`.
Examples:
 - header.webp -> ../CEDER Group at Berkeley and LBL_files/header.webp
 - IMG_2719-scaled-e1761102780887.jpg -> ../CEDER Group at Berkeley and LBL_files/IMG_2719-scaled-e1761102780887.jpg

To actually copy binary images, copy them from the original folder into this folder.
